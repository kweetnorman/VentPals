# VentPals Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2026

VentPals ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our app and website. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

---

## 1. Who we are

VentPals is a family-guided emotional wellness app designed to help children explore their feelings, develop calming skills, and build emotional resilience — with trusted grown-ups as supportive partners.

**Contact:** [support@ventpals.com](mailto:support@ventpals.com)

---

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Information you provide

| Data | Why we collect it |
|------|-------------------|
| Child's chosen username and display name | To personalise the in-app experience |
| 4-digit PIN (stored as a hash on-device) | To authenticate the child without a password |
| Parent/guardian email address | So a grown-up can link to a child's account |
| Connection code (VP-XXXXXX) | To link a child account to a trusted grown-up |
| Bird companion choice and avatar customisation | To remember the child's preferences |

### 2.2 Information we do NOT collect

- We do **not** collect real names, home addresses, phone numbers, or photos from children.
- We do **not** run third-party advertising in the app.
- We do **not** sell personal information to third parties.
- We do **not** use behavioural tracking or cross-app advertising profiles.

### 2.3 Automatically collected data

When you use the app or visit our website, our hosting provider may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser/OS type, pages visited). This data is used only for security monitoring and aggregate analytics, and is not linked to individual identities.

---

## 3. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K notice)

VentPals is designed for use by children with parental involvement. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

- **Account creation for children** is designed to require only a username and PIN. No email address or real name is required from the child.
- **Parent/guardian accounts** require an email address used solely for account linking and support.
- Parents may request deletion of their child's account and all associated data at any time by contacting [support@ventpals.com](mailto:support@ventpals.com).

If you believe a child has provided us with information we should not have, please contact us immediately.

---

## 4. How we use information

- To provide and improve the VentPals experience.
- To allow children and trusted grown-ups to link accounts securely.
- To send optional daily check-in reminder notifications (only if the user enables them; stored locally on the device).
- To respond to support requests.

---

## 5. Data storage and security

- **On-device data:** User preferences, PIN hash, and session data are stored in the device's local storage. This data never leaves the device unless you explicitly back it up.
- **Cloud data (Firebase):** Where account-linking features are used, data is stored in Google Firebase under Google's security standards. See [Firebase Privacy & Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).
- We use HTTPS for all network communication.

---

## 6. Sharing with third parties

We do not share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We may share data only:

- With service providers (e.g., Firebase/Google) strictly to operate the app.
- When required by law or to protect safety.

---

## 7. External links

VentPals may contain links to external websites (e.g., crisis resources, mental health organisations). These links open in an in-app browser or the system browser. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

---

## 8. Notifications

Daily check-in reminders are **entirely optional** and processed locally on your device. We do not use push notification services that transmit data to our servers (for v1).

---

## 9. Data deletion

To delete your data:

1. Open **Settings** in the app → scroll to the bottom → tap **Delete My Account** (coming in a future update).
2. Or email [support@ventpals.com](mailto:support@ventpals.com) with the subject "Delete my VentPals data" and we will process your request within 30 days.

---

## 10. Changes to this policy

We will notify users of material changes via an in-app notice. Continued use of VentPals after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
